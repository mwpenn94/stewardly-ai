# Stewardly AI — Quickstart Guide

**Last updated**: April 14, 2026

This guide walks a first-time user through the platform in under five minutes. Every step assumes you are starting from the login screen.

---

## Step 1: Sign In (30 seconds)

Navigate to the app URL and click **Sign In**. Stewardly uses Manus OAuth — no separate password to remember. If you prefer to explore first, you can use the platform as a **Guest** with limited features.

| Feature | Guest | Signed In |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| AI Chat (general education) | Yes | Yes |
| Personalized advice | No | Yes |
| Conversation history | No | Yes |
| Document uploads | No | Yes |
| Suitability assessment | No | Yes |
| Calculator sessions (save/load) | No | Yes |
| Knowledge base | No | Yes |
| Connected accounts | No | Yes |

---

## Step 2: Complete the Onboarding Tour (2 minutes)

When you first sign in, a **Spotlight Tour** automatically appears. It walks you through 14 key features across three categories:

1. **Getting Started** — sidebar navigation, AI chat, search
2. **Key Features** — Wealth Engine, calculators, documents, learning
3. **Personalization** — settings, suitability, AI tuning

Click **Next** to advance through each step, or **Skip** to dismiss. You can restart the tour anytime from the Help page.

---

## Step 3: Chat with Your AI Financial Twin (1 minute)

The **Chat** page is your primary interface. Type any financial question and the AI responds with personalized guidance. Key capabilities:

- **General education** — "What is a Roth IRA?" or "Explain dollar-cost averaging"
- **Personalized analysis** — "How should I allocate my portfolio?" (requires suitability profile)
- **Calculator integration** — "Run a retirement projection for me"
- **Document analysis** — Upload PDFs and ask questions about them
- **Multi-model synthesis** — Toggle ensemble mode for cross-verified answers

The AI uses **contextual intelligence** — it references your financial profile, uploaded documents, conversation history, and connected accounts to provide tailored responses.

---

## Step 4: Explore the Wealth Engine (1 minute)

Navigate to **Wealth Engine** in the sidebar. This is your complete financial planning toolkit:

| Section | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Quick Quote** | Instant multi-line insurance proposals (life, DI, LTC, P&C, umbrella) |
| **Strategy Compare** | Side-by-side comparison of 8 financial strategies |
| **Owner Comp** | S-Corp vs LLC vs C-Corp compensation optimization |
| **Financial Twin** | Full digital twin simulation of your financial life |

Below the Quick Bundle section, you will find **28 individual calculators** organized into three pillars:

- **Plan** — Retirement, tax projection, estate planning, education funding, social security, income protection, IUL, premium finance, Monte Carlo, financial twin
- **Protect** — Quick quoting, protection scorecard, insurance needs, risk assessment, strategy comparison
- **Grow** — Engine dashboard, owner compensation, business planning, implementation timeline, partner earnings, practice tracker, income streams

Each calculator has an **Export PDF** button that generates a branded report with compliance disclaimers.

---

## Step 5: Set Up Your Profile (1 minute)

Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar. Complete these tabs for the best experience:

1. **Profile & Style** — Set your name, avatar, and communication preferences
2. **Financial Profile** — Complete the suitability assessment (risk tolerance, time horizon, goals)
3. **AI Tuning** — Customize the 5-layer AI personalization cascade
4. **Connected Accounts** — Link LinkedIn, Google, or email for profile enrichment

The sidebar organizes features into persona-based sections:

| Section | Features |
|---------|----------|

| **Clients** | My Financial Twin, Insights, Suitability, Operations, Workflows, Client Onboarding |

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Press **?** at any time to see all keyboard shortcuts. Key ones:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `?` | Show keyboard shortcuts |
| `/` | Focus search |
| `g` then `h` | Go to home/chat |
| `g` then `s` | Go to settings |
| `g` then `c` | Go to calculators |
| `g` then `d` | Go to documents |

## For Advisors: Customize Your Organization's Branding

If you manage an advisory firm, navigate to **Settings → Org Branding Editor** to create a white-labeled experience for your clients:

| Tab | What You Can Customize |
|-----|------------------------|
| Content | Headline, subtitle, CTA button text, trust signals, disclaimer |
| Colors & Logo | Logo URL, primary/accent/secondary colors with visual swatches |
| Fonts | Choose from 12 Google Fonts with live specimen preview |
| Media | Hero image and favicon with preview |
| Advanced | Background pattern (mesh, dots, lines, radial, solid) + custom CSS |

Use the **desktop/mobile preview toggle** to see your changes in real-time before saving. Your branded landing page will be available at `/org/your-slug`.

## Getting Help

- **In-app**: Navigate to **Help** in the sidebar for searchable FAQ, feature guides, and contact form
- **Contextual**: Hover over any `?` icon for inline help tooltips
- **AI**: Ask the chat AI any question about the platform itself

---

## What Makes Stewardly Different

Stewardly is not a generic chatbot. It is a **digital financial twin** that:

1. **Remembers** — AI memory persists across conversations, building a deeper understanding of your financial life over time
2. **Learns** — The knowledge base ingests your documents and uses TF-IDF relevance scoring to surface the most pertinent information
3. **Adapts** — The 5-layer personalization cascade adjusts tone, complexity, and focus to match your communication style
4. **Verifies** — Multi-model synthesis queries multiple AI perspectives and cross-validates answers for accuracy
5. **Complies** — Every response includes appropriate disclaimers and follows Reg BI documentation standards

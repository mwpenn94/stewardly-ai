Funnel & Conversion Metrics — Step-by-Step Rate Benchmarks
How to read this section: Each funnel step below maps directly to the Sales Funnel tab inputs. Use these benchmarks to validate or adjust your rates. Approach→Set = % of contacts who agree to a meeting. Set→Held = % of scheduled meetings that actually happen. Held→App = % of held meetings that result in an application. App→Placed = % of submitted applications that become in-force policies (includes underwriting). Your overall conversion = all four rates multiplied together.

Business Income Engine (BIE)
Models personal production (GDC × bracket rate), team overrides (Gen1 + Gen2 cascade), affiliate income (Tracks A-D), AUM trail, channel marketing ROI, partner streams, renewal/trail income, and bonuses with configurable monthly seasonality patterns across 7 hierarchy roles.
Holistic Integration Engine (HE)
Combines BIE and UWE simulations. Business income feeds savings contributions (net income × savings rate), which feed product growth, which compounds with tax savings reinvestment. All value channels flow forward/back, roll-up/roll-down across all hierarchy levels.
Monte Carlo Simulation
1,000-trial simulation with randomized annual returns using normal distribution (Box-Muller transform). Default 15% annual standard deviation consistent with US equity markets. Returns capped -40% to +60%/yr. Produces p10/p25/p50/p75/p90 confidence bands. Source: Morningstar 2025 SBBI →
